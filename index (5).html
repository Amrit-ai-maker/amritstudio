About the Studio

We Don't Just Build Websites.
We Build Growth Machines.

It started the way most things do — out of frustration. A few years ago, Amrit watched relatives and local shop owners spend lakhs on poorly-made websites that brought them zero customers. The sites looked outdated, loaded slowly on cheap phones, and were built by agencies that had already moved on to the next client the moment the invoice was paid.

That became the mission: build digital systems that actually work for Indian businesses — fast, affordable, and designed for the real market. Today, AMRITSTUDIO.CO combines website design, AI-generated ad creatives, marketing automation, and growth strategy under one roof. Every client gets Amrit's personal attention on WhatsApp, not an outsourced team.

The advantage is simple: we use the latest AI tools — OpenAI, Midjourney, Sora, HeyGen — while competitors still work in the old way. That means your brand gets cinematic-quality ad creatives and a high-converting website at a fraction of the cost of a traditional agency. And because the studio is remote-first, you're never waiting on a physical office schedule.
